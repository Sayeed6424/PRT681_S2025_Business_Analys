Certificates for BA

**Agile Analysis Certification (AAC) – by IIBA**

→ Great for Agile teams; focuses on user stories \& collaboration

→ Learn via: iiba.org, LinkedIn Learning



**ISTQB CTBA (Certified Tester – Business Analyst)**

→ Combines testing \& BA skills; ideal for hybrid roles

→ Learn via: istqb.org, Udemy



**Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) – by Scrum Alliance**

→ Covers backlog, user stories, stakeholder needs

→ Learn via: scrumalliance.org, LinkedIn Learning
